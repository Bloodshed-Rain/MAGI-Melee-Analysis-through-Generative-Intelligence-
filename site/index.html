What it does

Your .slp files are denser than you think.

MAGI parses every frame of every replay — inputs, positions, state transitions, DI vectors, ledge options, punish windows. Not sampling. Every frame, across all 26 characters. Then it runs the numbers and surfaces what actually matters to your improvement.

Correlations you'd never find watching VODs.

Full pairwise Pearson correlation across 21 performance axes. MAGI doesn't just count — it finds relationships between metrics and explains what they mean for your character, matchup, and habits.

AI coaching

Six LLM providers. Your API key. No middleman.

OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Groq, Mistral, OpenRouter — bring your own key. Every prompt is built with matchup context, character frame data, and the statistical fingerprint of your session. Ask why you're losing the Fox matchup and get an answer in terms you'd hear from a top player doing commentary, not a chatbot telling you to "practice more."

Architecture

Desktop app. Local data. Open source.

Your replays stay on your machine. Analysis lives in a local SQLite database. Nothing phones home. Nothing requires an account. The codebase is MIT-licensed — fork it, extend it, break it apart. This is a tool, not a service.
